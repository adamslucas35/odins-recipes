Classic Meatball Sandwiches

Ingredients:

  • Precooked meatballs
  • 1/2 lb ground sausage
  • 1/2 onion chopped
  • 1 small bell pepper chopped
  • 1 can tomato sauce (8 ounces)
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon parsley
  • 1 teaspoon garlic salt
  • Hotdog buns (or whatever bread you would like

Description:

  1. Heat medium pan on medium, mix sausage, chopped onions, and chopped bell pepper until sausage is thoroughly cooked.
  2. Turn heat to a simmer, add the rest of the ingredients while stirring. (Add as many meatballs as you feel necessary to mix well with the sauce).
  3. Slowly stir until meatballs are fully warmed.
  4. Scoop 3-4 meatballs in between the two buns and enjoy. Optional: Add slices of cheese.
