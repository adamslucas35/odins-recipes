Authentic Italian Crepes

Ingredients:

  • 3 eggs
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup flour
  • Splash of water
  • Optional toppings: banana, nutella, peanut butter, maple syrup

Description:

  1. In small bowl (or blender) mix all ingredients. (only add water if you feel the mixture has become thick)

  2. Turn stove to medium heat. Place large frying pan on top to begin warming.

  3. Pour small circle of batter into the frying pan. Using the frying pan, spread the batter to fill complete bottom of pan.

  4. Flip half-cooked crepe with a turner once the bubbles have ceased popping.

  5. Cook other side of crepe until no longer stuck to pan.

  6. Let cool for 30 seconds, serve with topping of your liking.
